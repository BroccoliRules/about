Alexander J White

A biproduct from the marriage of James and Tamara White, Alex was born in Preston Idaho on January 12, 1994.

Although not popular enough to be featured in the popular film Napolean Dynamite, Alex has still done many interesting things in his 26 years of life.

High School

Alex graduated in 2012. He not only was active in playing soccer and lacrosse, but also started a ping pong club at his school.

BYU

Despite everyone telling him that Utah State was the place to be, Alex decided to go to BYU.
He started out studying Computer Science but quickly found that coding wasn't his thing.
Soon enough, he came to the light side and will graduate in Civil Engineering this April 2020.
He will graduate a single man, to his mother's despair. How ever will he find a good Mormon girl now....
